Enrichment — Diversity, Difficulty & Engagement

Enrichment is not one task. It spans a diverse set of data types of uneven difficulty, on a base where only a fraction is individually measurable today. This is the context behind the KPI targets.

170,501CRM contacts
All CRM records — mostly phone / SMS only. not on LINE Individually reachable by SMS, but not pushable or measurable on LINE.
50,000LINE followers
Reachable on LINE via broadcast. aggregate only Engagement measurable in aggregate — but anonymous until identified.
6,227identified
LINE UID + CRM profile — pushable to the individual. fully measurable The only base where engagement is measured per grower today.
Enrichment gap: ~43,773 blank followers (reachable but anonymous) to identify · 164,274 SMS-only contacts to convert to LINE. Identified base = 12.5% of followers · 3.7% of CRM.
